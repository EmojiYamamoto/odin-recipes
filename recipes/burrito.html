Burrito

These easy beef burritos come together in no time and are loaded with spices, vegetables and flavor! A simple, healthier and easy dinner idea that you need in your life!

  • Ground beef
  • Spices (cumin, black pepper, chili flakes and oregano)
  • Vegetables (cauliflower, bell peppers and zucchini)
  • Sauce (any of preference)

How to make

  1. Start by making the filling. Cook your beef with olive oil and spices you enjoy. As I mentioned I used cumin, chili flakes, black pepper and oregano. In 4-5 minutes add in the bell pepper, some garlic, some scallions, zucchini and thinly chopped cauliflower. Reduce the heat and cover with a lid to allow the ingredients to cook through for a few minutes. Stir in the spinach, add some tomato paste and a squeeze of lemon. Turn the heat off.
  2. Assemble the burritos. Place the ground beef filling in the middle of a tortilla, then some cheese on top, yogurt garlic sauce (or another sauce of choice) and guacamole on top and fold the tortilla on each side like an envelope.
  3. Toast the burrito in a nonstick pan with some olive oil until golden brown. This will make it crispier and help seal the tortilla, so that the filling stays inside. Enjoy immediately.
